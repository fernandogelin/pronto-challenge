Pronto Cycle Share

a year of rides in Seattle

Ridership by group

Between October 13, 2014 and October 12, 2015, Pronto users went for over 140,000 rides and spent over 48,000 hours cycling. A quick look at the data reveals annual members and day-pass holders use Pronto bikes in very dinstinct ways. While annual members are responsible for 60% of the trips, day-pass holders keep their bikes for longer periods and are responsible for almost 70% of the total time bikes were in use. The average trip duration is about 10 minutes for annual members and 35 minutes for day-pass holders.
